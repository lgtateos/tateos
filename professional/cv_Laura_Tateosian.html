RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

Postdoctoral Research Associate

January 2007 - Present

Center for Earth Observation
North Carolina State University, NC
Working with subject experts to solve data management and visualization problems in applications such as forestry, fire management, national heritage site inventory, and natural resources monitoring.

Developing and teaching core courses for the new Master’s in Geospatial Information Science and Technology (GIST) degree at NCSU. Topics include the mathematical and algorithmic foundations of spatial analysis. Students also learn to automate data preprocessing and analysis, and visualize the results. Student projects address real-world governmental and industrial geospatial challenges.

Investigating the advantages of using painterly techniques to visualize spatial datasets. Building on my thesis work, we are studying viewer engagement with aesthetic visualizations and developing comparative studies for various visualization techniques.
